Iteration Builds Momentum

August 2015

Last week we published our new mission and it's already jump started new conversations at ZURB. It's cool to see how a few words can have an impact on the way conversations unfold. It's even better knowing that they help create those words. At ZURB it really is important that our team buy into our company values because they form a foundation of our shared learning.

Our team is really committed to getting better every day. You say...who isn't though? In actuality, very few teams really commit to improving their work. It's hard to do and more importantly it takes practicing as a team for everyone to grow. When we share a common vision, learning and accepting feedback becomes so much easier and rewarding. It's amazing to be part of something that creates meaningful impact.

We've committed ourselves to change the way people design connected products and services globally. 50 Million people in fact.That's a lofty goal and only achievable with a focused team willing to challenge itself everyday to learn. We've put high expectations on ourselves knowing that these goals will only make us stronger. On some days they might make us tired, stressed and exhausted- but it's not a short term race- patience and heart are required to achieve the mission.

I'm 17 years into this ZURB journey and I'm incredibly lucky to be doing it with a group of passionate people who understand that change is the norm and great things happen when we stretch ourselves to help others achieve amazing things. I thank my team for the incredible opportunity to do what I love and lead this team.

Now, on to the news!

-Bryan

ZURB Wired 2015: Downtown Streets Team

Now in our 8th year, ZURB Wired is a chance to give back to our community and make a positive impact. Each year we select a local nonprofit that's doing amazing things and mobilize our team to craft an entire marketing campaign that includes a brand new responsive website, posters, postcards, tshirts, videos and more. The crazy part? We do it all in 24 straight hours. Yep, we work all night!

We're proud to announce we've selected Downtown Streets Team for this year's ZURB Wired! Downtown Streets Team is ending homelessness in Silicon Valley by providing people the tools, training, and support to transform their lives permanently.

Starting at 8 a.m.(PST), August 27th straight through until 8 a.m.(PST), you'll be able to follow our progress on the ZURB Wired Site and our @ZURB Twitter Handle (Be sure to use the #ZURBWired hashtag to cheer us on!).
